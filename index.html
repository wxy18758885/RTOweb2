    Four personalized courses

      Characteristic course

      • suitable for students •

      students with weak foundation and unable to follow class.

      • Course features •

      sort out knowledge, train learning methods, consolidate foundation and build knowledge system.

      Special course

      • suitable for students •

      students who are weak and insufficient

      • Course features •

      focus on the weak links one by one, training methods, make up for the weak points and consolidate the foundation.

      Potential course

      • suitable for students •

      students with short learning time and weak foundation

      • Course features •

      follow the concept of personalized learning, teach students according to their aptitude, adjust measures to time and teach learning methods according to the current situation of short learning time and weak foundation of individual students.

      Combing class

      • suitable for students •

      students who need to consolidate and sort out their knowledge.

      • Course features •

      carefully design course contents, teach learning methods and sort out knowledge structure through data analysis.
